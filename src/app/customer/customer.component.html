Welcome to Railway Ticket App Support

Please refer to the following frequently asked questions and troubleshooting tips. If you still need assistance, feel free to contact our support team.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I book a ticket using the app?

    To book a ticket using our app, follow these steps:

    1. Open the app and login or sign up if you haven't already.
    2. Search for your desired destination and date of travel.
    3. Select the train and class of travel.
    4. Enter your personal and payment details.
    5. Confirm your booking.

  • I'm unable to login to the app. What should I do?

    If you're having trouble logging into the app, try the following:

    1. Make sure you're using the correct username and password.
    2. Check your internet connection.
    3. Try resetting your password by clicking on the "forgot password" link on the login page.

    If the issue persists, please contact our support team for assistance.

  • I've booked a ticket, but I haven't received a confirmation email or SMS. What should I do?

    If you haven't received a confirmation email or SMS after booking a ticket, try the following:

    1. Check your spam or junk folder in your email inbox.
    2. Make sure you entered the correct email and phone number during booking.
    3. Wait for a few minutes as it may take some time for the confirmation to be sent.

    If you still haven't received a confirmation, please contact our support team for assistance.

  • I want to cancel my ticket. How do I do that?

    To cancel a ticket, follow these steps:

    1. Open the app and go to your bookings.
    2. Select the ticket you want to cancel.
    3. Click on the "cancel" button.
    4. Confirm the cancellation.

    Please note that cancellation charges may apply depending on the time of cancellation and the fare rules.

Troubleshooting Tips

If you're experiencing any issues while using the app, try the following troubleshooting tips:

  1. Make sure you're using the latest version of the app.
  2. Check your internet connection.
  3. Restart the app or your device.
  4. Clear the cache and cookies of the app.
  5. If none of the above works, uninstall and reinstall the app.
